# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

Be respectful, specific, and constructive. Welcome people with different levels of video, AI, and software experience. Critique ideas and implementations without attacking people.

Harassment, discriminatory language, threats, doxxing, sexualized attention, and publishing another person's private voice, likeness, credentials, or client material are not acceptable.

## Scope and enforcement

This standard applies in repository discussions, issues, pull requests, reviews, and project-related community spaces. Maintainers may edit, hide, reject, or remove contributions that violate it and may temporarily or permanently restrict participation.

Report conduct concerns privately to the maintainers through the repository's available private contact channel. Do not disclose another person's sensitive information in a public issue.
